What is this?

URStudying was an app I made because I felt that students who were in the same classes needed a better way to connect with each other. Especially in the larger classes, I saw this as an issue because there's no real time to talk to your peers in lecture, and once it's over, many students are too exahusted to try and set up study groups. Not to mention connecting with students in other sections of the same course, etc. I hope this app can facilitate this process and connect students quickly and easily so we can all get higher grades in our courses and hopefully make some new friends along the way!

How do I use this P.O.S site?

Wow, ouch.Ok.
To create a group:If you have a group or if you're looking to make one press the "I have a study group already/I'm looking to create a study group" button and follow the prompts. After that other students can search and find your group!
To edit a group:Press the "I already made a group; let me edit it!" button and you'll see the list of posts you made. If you go by different usernames for different classes, or just decide to change it at some point, the groups under your old username won't show up anymore. This is a security feature more than anything. So, use the same username for all the groups you make! The edit page will also only show groups that are CURRENTLY active. To change group size, click on the number and change it to the new size. To change the times you'll be studying, click the time you want to change (start or end) and change it to a new time in a "12:00pm" format. To change the body notes, expand the info and click the text you wrote. To cancel, simply hit the cancel group button. Finally, after you've finished your edits, click "Submit Edits" and you're done!
To search for a group:Click the "I'm looking for a study group" button and follow the prompt!
